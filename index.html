I'm an associate professor at the Mathematics and Computer Science Department at Virginia State University.

My current research interests are centered around software maintenance, especially when applied to non-mainstream software domains, such as high-performance computing. I'm actively looking for motivated graduate and undergraduate students to join my research group. Please contact me if you are interested.
